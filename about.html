about us

It has been 5 years since the formation of the Kerala Drivers Chunk Bros (KDCB) movement.The main objective of this movement is to work for the upliftment and growth of the drivers. And now, KDCB has became a .... front-runner for any of the chunks, with the motto that loopers will not be left alone on the road. The KDCB has WhatsApp groups in 14 districts, as well as in 6 GCC countries & in Bangalore. Currently we have a KDCB Facebook group of 135,000 members. There is also a 24-hour emergency group.The activities of GCC groups stand apart from the rest of KDCB. One of the highlight is the activities of the KDCB Saudi Group. KDCB Saudi Group has Charity Activities and Blood Donation Camps.
